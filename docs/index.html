Study 4 · 3 regimes, supply-side

The Disclosure Decision Under Regimes

Do stigma / benefit regimes change whether writers truthfully label their own AI use? Gallery (regime carrier) → writing with a logged AI assistant → regime revealed → label choice. Truthful disclosure given logged use is the primary outcome.

污名/受益规制是否会改变写作者是否如实标注自己对 AI 的使用？展廊（规制载体）→ 使用带记录的 AI 助手写作 → 揭示规制 → 标签选择。如实披露（在有 AI 使用记录的前提下）是主要结果。
